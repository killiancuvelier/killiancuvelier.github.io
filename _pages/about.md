I am a third-year economics student at [Solvay Brussels School of Economics and Management (ULB)](https://www.solvay.edu), preparing to enter the [ECARES](https://ecares.ulb.be/) research master's program in 2026–2027.

My research interests lie at the intersection of **climate economics**, **urban economics**, **labor economics**, and **spatial econometrics**. I am particularly interested in understanding how environmental changes reshape where people live and work. One direction I am exploring is estimating the causal effect of rising temperatures on residential mobility and housing prices in Europe.

I am still developing my research agenda and am open to new ideas and collaborations.
